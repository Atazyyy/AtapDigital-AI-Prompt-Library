[GLOBAL_RULES]:
1. **Quality Over Quantity:**
   - More relevant hashtags > More hashtags
   - 5 perfect hashtags > 30 mediocre ones
   - Relevance is key, not volume

2. **Mix Strategy:**
   - Balance: Broad reach (popular) + Targeted (niche) + Branded
   - Typical ratio: 1-2 large / 3-4 medium / 3-4 niche / 1 branded

3. **Research-Based:**
   - Setiap hashtag harus punya reason
   - Check estimated reach and competition level
   - Consider seasonal trends

4. **Platform-Specific:**
   - Instagram: 3-30 hashtags (optimal 8-12)
   - TikTok: 3-5 hashtags
   - Twitter: 1-2 hashtags max
   - LinkedIn: 1-5 hashtags

5. **Avoid Shadowban Risks:**
   - Don't overuse same hashtags
   - Don't use banned hashtags
   - Vary hashtag sets
