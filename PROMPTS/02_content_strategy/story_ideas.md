### 7. 🎯 Story Best Practices

#### DO ✅
- Post at consistent times
- Use all interactive features
- Respond to DMs from stories
- Save best stories to highlights
- Mix content types
- Include clear CTAs
- Show your face (builds trust)

#### DON'T ❌
- Post random, unconnected content
- Over-promote (max 20% promo)
- Ignore engagement
- Use low quality visuals
- Skip highlights organization
- Post TOO frequently
- Forget to track analytics
