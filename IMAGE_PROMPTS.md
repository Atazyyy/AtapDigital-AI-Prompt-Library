## 🎨 Style Keywords

Tambahkan di akhir prompt sesuai preferensi:

| Style | Keywords |
|-------|----------|
| **Glassmorphism** | `glassmorphism, frosted glass, backdrop blur, translucent panels` |
| **Neon Glow** | `neon glow, light bloom, volumetric lighting, ambient glow` |
| **Minimalist** | `minimalist, clean, simple, lots of negative space` |
| **Futuristic** | `futuristic, sci-fi, holographic, cyberpunk elements` |
| **Professional** | `professional, corporate, clean typography, business ready` |
| **Modern** | `modern, contemporary, sleek, cutting-edge design` |

## ❌ Things to Avoid

- Teks/typography langsung (generate tanpa text, tambah teks sendiri)
- Terlalu ramai/kompleks
- Logo brand lain
- Orang/wajah (kecuali mau human-centric design)
